As Simone Biles competes in the all-around team final for women’s gymnastics at the Paris 2024 Olympics today, you can purchase some of the record-breaking Olympian’s favorite activewear on Athleta, which is currently having a sitewide sale.

Biles’ favorite activewear, from leggings to running shorts, is featured in Athleta’s “Power of She” collective, which “celebrates, and empowers game-changing women,” according to their website. The campaign is fronted by dozens of Olympians, such as Biles, Katie Ledecky, Monica Puig and Heather O’Reilly with performance insights that drive product development in a lineup of their favorite pieces.
