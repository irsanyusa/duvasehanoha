Quiz: Which Hogwarts House You Truly Belong In

June 07, 2024
blog

23 years ago, J.K. Rowling’s first book in the Harry Potter series, Harry Potter and the Philosopher’s Stone, was published. Its film adaptation, under the title Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone, was released 19 years ago. Since then, possibly the most important question a young millennial can pose is “Which Hogwarts house do I belong to?” However, since we are all complex beings, it may seem as though a single house cannot fully represent our personalities in their entirety. That being said, here is a Hogwarts house quiz that delves a bit deeper.

And now it’s time for you to count how many times you chose Gryffindor, Ravenclaw, Hufflepuff, or Slytherin. The Hogwarts house that you’ve chosen the most is the one you belong in.
