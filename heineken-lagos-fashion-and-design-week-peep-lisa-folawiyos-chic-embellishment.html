Peep Lisa Folawiyo's chic embellishment overload for day 3 of LDFW 2016

August 17, 2024
blog

Lisa Folawiyo's collection is always a joy to look forward to as she combines timeless looks effortlessly without missing out on the fun and elegance her ultimate muse should bask in. Chic silhouettes, clean lines, unmissable details as embellished knits with sequin plates as tassels, ruffles, waterfall hemlines, defined cinch to make the whole look come altogether effortless but timelessly elegant.

Working prints for Lisa Folawiyo gives life, hope to the hopeless romantic who see herself wherever she wants in her head with the dreamy, sultry pieces that can compete with any piece anywhere in the world.

ADVERTISEMENT

Her designs reminds one of a Chanel meets Victoria Beckham type of timeless luxury with nothing out of place as simple as it comes off. The ultimate LF woman has to love her travels, luxury and bask in them and the collection made that happen.

Lush dresses featuring cinch definition, just enough bling, cage crotchets, sultry peek-a-boo, the muse is in tune with her own sexuality and all round confident.

The revered designer closed the show and she shouldn't have done it better, definitely the best and the highlight of the last day, I won't forget the joy that filled my heart after I watched her close (I sat up looking intently through each piece off the showcase).
