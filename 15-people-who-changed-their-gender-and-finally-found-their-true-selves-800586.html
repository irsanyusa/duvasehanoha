While some people only need a visit to a hairstylist or a nail artist to attain the necessary changes to their appearance and feel happy, others require a bit more than that. Gender transition is something we are going to talk about today. It is a life-long decision that requires not only a lot of forethought and weighing of all the pros and cons, but also immense courage and bravery.

We at Bright Side admire people who listen to their heart and do what it asks them to do. That’s why, today, we compiled a list 15+ people who were brave enough to start the long journey toward gender transition.
