July 05, 2024

The appearance of stars is an important part of their personal brand. This is why nobody of them likes it when they are compared to or even confused with another famous person. But there’s not much they can do about the fact that, scientifically speaking, there’s a high chance to have a look-alike somewhere in the world. The only truly surprising thing is how they managed to become popular in the same field.

The people from today’s Bright Side compilation have often been on the lists of celebrities that look a lot like each other. But is it really true? Let’s take a closer look at them.
