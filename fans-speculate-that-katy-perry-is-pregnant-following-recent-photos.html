American Idol judge Katy Perry is the latest celebrity at the center of pregnancy rumors, due to photos of the singer attending the recent Billboard Women in Music Awards. Some fans are speculating that the singer might be expecting her second child with her fiancé Orlando Bloom.

Is Katy Perry Pregnant with her Second Child?

Last week, Perry attended the Billboard Women in Music Awards wearing an eye-catching red outfit. The look featured a strapless corset top and long skirt. Notably, the back of Perry’s skirt had the same lacing as her top, showing a flash of her backside with a black thong peeking out.

However, it was the front of Perry’s outfit that sparked chatter about a possible pregnancy. The singer’s belly was exposed in the low-riding skirt. According to The Sun, some fans wondered if Perry might have a baby bump.

As one Instagram user commented on People‘s post about her outfit, “Pregnant?” The comment has received over a thousand likes. However, some fans replied calling it rude to scrutinize Perry’s body.

“Sometimes after having a baby your belly doesn’t return to being flat FYI,” wrote another commenter on the same post. Perry welcomed her daughter Daisy Dove with her fiancé Orlando Bloom in 2020.

Perry Has Been Photographed More Recently

In the days since the Women in Music event, Perry has been photographed in various outfits. The Sun published photos of the star wearing a gray sweatsuit while out and about in Beverly Hills.

Over the weekend, Perry wore a casual outfit of jeans and a blazer. Perry also attended a pre-Oscars party wearing a body-hugging white dress with a gold belt. The singer definitely doesn’t seem to be hiding her figure, whether or not she’s expecting.

Perry recently announced that the current season of American Idol would be her last. A source told The Mirror that her decision was partly influenced by the desire to expand her family.
