America’s Got Talent: All-Stars is currently winding down, as the finale is scheduled for next Monday night, following performances from the 11 finalists in this week’s episode. The finale will feature several famous guest performers, including a few past AGT contestants.

AGT: All-Stars Finale to Include Famous Guest Performers

On Monday, February 27, the winner of AGT: All-Stars will be announced in a two-hour finale. The finalists will perform alongside some famous guests, including the band Weezer and singer Babyface. American Idol alum Adam Lambert is also among the finale performers.

The finale will also feature several past AGT contestants, including violinist Lindsay Stirling and winning magician Mat Franco. The episode will also include AGT: All-Stars alums Terry Fator and Voices of Hope Children’s Choir.

Last month, Fator teased on social media that he would be making an appearance in the finale, leading fans to speculate that young ventriloquist Ana-Maria Mărgean would be a finalist even before her first performance on the spin-off.

Who Will Be Named the Winner?

Eleven acts are competing in the AGT: All-Stars Finals this week, but only one of them can be named the winner according to the Superfan vote. Will it be one of the five Golden Buzzer acts chosen by the judges and host? Or will another act take the title?

My prediction is that singer Kodi Lee will be named the ultimate All-Star in next week’s finale. Lee’s inspiring Golden Buzzer audition went viral back in Season 14, and his incredible talent earned him the $1 million prize. During All-Stars, Lee didn’t need a Golden Buzzer to go to the Finals — the Superfans voted him through.
