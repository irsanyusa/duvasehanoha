If you would like to get in touch, we'd love to hear from you - just choose the appropriate contact information below. 

Visit

If you have any enquiries regarding any aspect of visiting Scotland contact VisitScotland.

Work

If you would like to know more about living and working in Scotland or need information about immigration and employment contact TalentScotland.

Study

If you are an international student interested in applying to a Scottish university and need some advice on your personal circumstances, please use our course search and/or contact each University Admission department individually, as they often will have different admission requirements. You can find information about the individual universities on our Universities in Scotland page.

Business

If you would like to know more about what Scotland has to offer as a business location or partner contact Scottish Development International.

Scotland in the USA

The Scottish Affairs Office in Washington DC exists to help promote Scotland's interests in the United States. It is based at the British Embassy, located at 3100 Massachusetts Ave NW, Washington DC 20008.

Scotland's Migration Service

Scotland's Migration Service aims to help people to settle in Scotland. It is delivered by a charity called Citizens Advice Scotland and a law firm called Seraphus. The service is paid for by the Scottish Government.

If you have a question about Scotland's Migration Service contact: migration@cas.org.uk.
