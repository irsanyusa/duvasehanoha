Hours before the Super Eagles clash with Angola, Amadu Raheem Komba Gbandeh released his prediction of the African Cup of Nations (AFCON) game. The young man had gone viral days ago for predicting the outcomes of seven AFCON matches without mistake.

Amadu shared his prediction in a fresh post on Facebook, which has generated a buzz on the social media platform.

The Sierra Leonean tipped Nigeria to win Angola and predicted Guinea would emerge victorious in their quarter-final match against DR Congo.
