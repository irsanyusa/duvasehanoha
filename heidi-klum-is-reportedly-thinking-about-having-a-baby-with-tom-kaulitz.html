Supermodel and America’s Got Talent judge Heidi Klum is reportedly planning to have a baby with her husband Tom Kaulitz. Currently at 49 years old, Klum admittedly wants to expand her blended family, while considering her age at the same time.

Heidi Klum Shares Desire to Have More Kids

Klum and her husband Kaulitz have been married since 2019 and the model now wants to take the next step in their relationship. At almost 50 years old, Klum carefully considers the difficulties of delivering a baby at her age.

“I mean wanting it and it actually being possible are always two different things,” Klum said. “The big 50 is just around the corner. And things get harder when you get older. But would I want it? Sometimes I think yes.”

While Klum admits her desire have a baby later in life, she does not sugarcoat the process of doing so. The supermodel is already a mother to four children, the eldest is her daughter Leni Klum, 18, who she had with her relationship with Flavio Briatore. Her other children, sons Henry, 17, Johan, 15, and daughter Lou, 13, with her ex-husband, Seal.

Even though Klum hasn’t created any of her children with her current husband, she’s shared that he’s stepped into the role of father. Klum celebrated Father’s Day by posting on Instagram and dedicating it to Kaulitz, emphasizing how important he is to her and her children. Klum appears very happy and contented with her life at the moment, but if having a baby after 50 is in the cards, then it looks like she’s up for it.

The Real Reason She Broke Up With Seal

Speaking of her ex-husband Seal, fans were initially perplexed and wondering why the divorce happened after nine years of marriage. At the time, Klum and Seal were one of the most well-known celebrity couples out there, drawing attention from their fame.

The two were originally tight-lipped when it comes to the reasons surrounding their divorce. They said that busy schedules were the main contributing factor to why they grew apart. Despite renewing their vows eight times in a span of seven years, it still wasn’t enough to save their marriage.

“We have had the deepest respect for one another throughout our relationship and continue to love each other very much.” Klum said in their first public statement.

Afterward, Klum revealed that Seal struggled with managing his anger which turned problematic with children at home. She claimed his “inability to control his anger,” was difficult to live with and it gradually became worse over time. They filed for divorce in 2012, and it became official in 2014, sources agreed that Seal’s anger issues were the main reason they couldn’t make it work.
