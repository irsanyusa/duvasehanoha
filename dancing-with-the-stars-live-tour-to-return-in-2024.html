The Dancing With The Stars Live Tour will finally be having its comeback. Set to kick off next year, it promises to be a spanking new show brimming with ultimate entertainment.

Dancing With The Stars to Embark on Tour Next Year

DWTS is not only having a season 32, but also gearing up for a mindblowing tour. Dancing pro Emma Slater announced the great news alongside fellow pro Britt Stewart in a comical video posted on Tuesday.

“Dazzling routines, jaw-dropping talent and non-stop entertainment will be coming to a city near you with an ALL-NEW SHOW!” the caption reads.

While Slater and Stewart revealed that the tour will happen in 2024, no further details were disclosed by the duo. According to the DWTS tour website, the upcoming tour has casted fan-favorites and even special guest stars.

Furthermore, the show introduces Mirrorball Membership, the first ever DWTS Live Tour fan club. Fans can sign up for free and get access to exclusive pre-sales. With the Mirrorball Membership, they can also receive new updates about tour dates, special guests, on-sale information, as well as sneak peek of rehearsals.

Emma Slater Teased Reunion with Britt Stewart Ahead of Announcement

Ahead of the live tour announcement, Slater shared on Instagram that she and Stewart met up for work. Not much was hinted at the time, but the two were holding mirror balls beside a hanging rack full of ballroom outfits.

“They said they needed someone with balls… Reunited with @brittbenae,” she wrote.

On July 11, Slater posted a slideshow of snaps on Instagram, showing some behind-the-scenes of their video shoot. Her caption then teased that a special announcement is coming.

DWTS fans are definitely excited to know that a live tour will take place soon. The show has already revealed the cities where the tour cast will perform. These cities include Amarillo, Texas; Bethlehem, Pennsylvania; Buffalo, New York and many others.
