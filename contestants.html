FULL NAME: Joseph Adam Jonas

HOMETOWN: Casa Grande, Arizona

BIRTHDAY: August 15, 1989

AGE: 35

RELATIONSHIP STATUS: Divorcing Sophie Turner

BIO: Joe Jonas rose to fame with his brothers, Kevin and Nick as part of The Jonas Brothers. Jonas launched his career after Columbia Records liked Nick’s debut single in 2022. Soon after, the record company formed The Jonas Brothers. The group later signed to Hollywood records, where they released their second album. The Jonas Brothers gained mass recognition when they broke into mainstream music through Disney Channel. Nick, Kevin, and Joe were in Camp Rock, Camp Rock 2: The Final Jam, as well as their own series, Jonas Brothers: Living the Dream and Jonas. Ahead of The Jonas Brothers’ break up, Jonas took on various acting roles and began recording a solo album. After the group officially disbanded in 2013, Jonas created his own group, DNCE. Around the same time, he joined the Australian version of The Voice. In the present day, The Jonas Brothers are back together and happier than ever. In 2023, the group earned a star on the Hollywood walk of Fame. Jonas is currently married to actress Sophie Turner, they have two children together.
